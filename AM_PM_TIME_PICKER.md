# ✅ AM/PM TIME PICKER - ADDED!

## 🕐 New Feature: 12-Hour Time Format with AM/PM

I've replaced the 24-hour time input with a beautiful AM/PM time picker!

### 🎨 What's New:

#### **3 Dropdown Selectors:**
1. **Hour** - 01 to 12
2. **Minute** - 00 to 59
3. **Period** - AM or PM

#### **Live Time Preview:**
- Shows selected time in green badge
- Format: "08:30 AM"
- Updates as you change selections

#### **Default Time:**
- Hour: 08
- Minute: 00
- Period: AM
- (8:00 AM)

### 🎯 How It Works:

1. **Select Hour**: Choose from 01-12
2. **Select Minute**: Choose from 00-59
3. **Select AM/PM**: Toggle between AM/PM
4. **See Preview**: Time displays below in green

### 💾 Backend Conversion:

The frontend automatically converts to 24-hour format before sending to backend:
- **08:30 AM** → `08:30`
- **02:15 PM** → `14:15`
- **12:00 PM** → `12:00` (noon)
- **12:00 AM** → `00:00` (midnight)
